NC-TF-3283

10/100Mbps 32-bit PCI Network Adapter


product features

  • Single chip PCI Bus 10/100Mbps Fast Ethernet adapter
  • Fully complies with IEEE 802.3u and 802.3 standards
  • Supports ACPI, PCI power management
  • 32-bit PCI Local Bus master architecture
  • Complies with PCI specification 2.1, 2.2, ACPI, and PCI Bus Power Management
  • Auto-setup IRQ and I/O address
