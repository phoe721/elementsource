IT-ES308

8-Ports 10/100Mbps Desktop Switch


product features

  • Fully complies with IEEE 802.3 and 802.3u Standards
  • IEEE 803.x flow control for full duplex
  • Back Pressure flow control for half duplex
  • Support Auto MDI/MDIX on each port
  • Wire speed reception and transmission
  • Store and forward switching method
  • Support 1K MAC address
  • Data 1M RAM buffering 
  • Extensive front panel diagnostic LEDs
