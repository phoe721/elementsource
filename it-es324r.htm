product features

  • Full or half duplex transfer mode for 10Mbps or 100Mbps
  • Wire speed reception and transmission
  • Store and forward switching method
  • Support TP Interface Auto MDIX function for Auto TX/RX Swap 
  • Extensive front panel diagnostic LEDs
  • Perform Broadcast Storming filter function
  • IEEE 802.3x flow control for full duplex
  • Industry standard 19” rack-mount size
