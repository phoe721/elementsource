Do you match competitor's prices?

We do match other competitor's prices. You can feel free to contact us for quantity.

Do you charge sales tax?

Element Source Inc. now operates business from locations in CA. California residents living within Los Angeles county will be charged 8.25%. If you live outside of Los Angeles county but in the state of California, a 7.25% tax will be applied. UPS ships to Hawaii, Alaska and Puerto Rico via UPS Ground, UPS 2nd Day and UPS Next Day Saver only. If you've selected any other UPS shipping method prior to choosing a shipping address in Hawaii, Alaska or Puerto Rico, your shipping method will default to the cheapest available UPS shipping method and you will be charged accordingly.

What are my payment choices?

Element Source Inc. currently offers the following payment methods: Credit Cards (U.S. Issued): Visa/Mastercard, Discover, American Express Debit Cards (U.S. Issued) with a Visa or Mastercard logo Prepaid Cashier Check/Money Order (U.S. Issued ) Prepaid Personal Check (U.S. Issued) Please allow approximately 5~7 business days for the check to clear. For wire transfer, please contact Element Source Inc. for account information.

What are my shipping method choices?

Standard Overnight- One business day
Two Day Air - Two business days
Three Day Air - Three business days
Ground Service- Approximately 5 business days

Can you ship to a different address than my billing?

Yes, we are able to ship to an address other than your billing address. However, we do require an authorization, so that we can help cut down on fraudulent usage of credit cards, that is currently so prevalent on the internet. This one step helps us cut down on fraud, keeps our costs down, and enables us to provide our customers with the lowest price possible. The procedure is done strictly to protect consumers, as well as ourselves as a merchant. We realize that this policy may be slightly inconvenient, however it enables us to provide you with the best possible price. We hope you understand our position, and thank you for your cooperation!

How can I cancel an order?

You may request a cancellation of your order, provided your order has not already been processed and shipped. Simply call (626)-382-5995 during Element Source Inc.'s hours of operation to request an order cancellation. We will do our very best to cancel the order, and will send a confirmation upon cancellation. If your account has already been charged, you can anticipate a refund to your account within 1-2 business days. We strongly discourage sending order cancellation requests via email because it is unlikely we will receive the message in time to void the order due to the sheer volume of emails we receive each day and for security purposes.
