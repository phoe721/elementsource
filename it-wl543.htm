IT-WL543

802.11g 54Mbps Wireless PCI Adapter


product features

  •  Adds 802.11g wireless capabilities to desktop computers, for faster wireless networking available for home or office
  • Compliant with PCI v. 2.2 specifications
  • Backward compatible with existing
    802.11b products
  • Capable of up to 256-bit WEP Encryption
  • Data transfer rate up to 54Mbps
  • Support Windows 98SE/2000/ME/XP
  • Lower Power Consumption
  • Easy to install and configure
